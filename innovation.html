Engineering Tomorrow

Pioneering Tech

At Sundial, innovation is not a department—it's our DNA. We push the boundaries of physics and materials science to redefine what a timepiece can do.

Solar-Kinetic Drive

A revolutionary movement that converts both ambient light and kinetic energy into stored power. With a 365-day power reserve, a Sundial never sleeps.

Quantum Link

Utilizing low-earth orbit satellite constellations, our watches synchronize with atomic clocks daily, maintaining accuracy to within one second every 30,000 years.

Diamond Shield

A proprietary Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) process applies a 5-micron layer of synthetic diamond to every case, making it virtually scratch-proof.

The Architecture of
Precision

Our proprietary 'Vortex' movement features over 420 individual components, each hand-finished to tolerances of less than 0.001mm. This is not just assembly; it's a microscopic ballet. The balance wheel oscillates at a high frequency of 36,000 vibrations per hour, providing a sweeping second hand motion that is as smooth as time itself.

This level of complexity allows for multiple high-complications including a perpetual calendar that requires no adjustment until the year 2100, a precision moon phase accurate to one day in 122 years, and a dual-timezone display that adjusts automatically via the Quantum Link system.

Material
Alchemy

We source only the most resilient materials from across the globe and beyond. Our 'Star-Dust' dials are crafted from slices of the Muonionalusta meteorite, while our cases use Grade 5 Titanium—the same alloy found in deep-space exploration vessels.

Every crystal is cut from laboratory-grown sapphire, reaching a Mohs hardness of 9, ensuring that your view of time remains crystal clear for generations.

Our Manufacturing Ethos

01. Micro-Engineering

Our CNC machines operate at tolerances of 0.5 microns, ensuring every gear teeth interlocks with mathematical perfection. This precision is what allows our movements to run for decades without service.

02. Hand-Finishing

Machine precision is only the start. Every bridge is hand-beveled (Anglage), and every screw is heat-blued to a specific shade of cobalt, a process that takes years to master.

03. Rigorous Testing

Each Sundial undergoes a 600-hour 'Cosmos Test'—exposed to extreme temperatures, magnetic fields of 15,000 Gauss, and pressure equivalent to 1000m depth.
